FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

How do I book a delivery?

BeeClub delivers anything across Jeddah. Book in the app, track your order, get it delivered today. We work 24/7. Price based on loading/unloading time. Drivers are verified.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept major credit and debit cards, including Visa and MasterCard. Digital wallets like Apple Pay and Google Pay are also supported. Cash on delivery is available for convenience.

How is the price calculated?

The total fee depends on the loading and unloading time required. Distance within Jeddah also factors into the cost. We aim to provide transparent and fair pricing for every order.

What areas do you cover?

Our delivery service covers all neighborhoods across Jeddah. We operate 24/7 to ensure your order reaches you anytime, anywhere in the city without delay.

Can I track my delivery?

Yes, you can track your delivery in real time through our app. This gives you live updates from pickup to drop-off, so you always know your order’s status.

What if I need help loading?

Our verified drivers can assist with loading and unloading. Just indicate your need for help when booking, and they will come prepared to support you safely.

Can I cancel my order?

You may cancel your order anytime before the driver arrives. Early cancellation helps avoid fees, but some charges may apply depending on timing and driver dispatch.
